Variables description

Prediction Outcomes

  • Hospital mortality
  • Major adverse kidney events (MAKE) : the composite of death, dependence on renal replacement therapy and a drop in eGFR ≥50% from baseline up to 120 days from hospital discharge. See reference below for details.

Demographics/Vitals

  • Age : Age of patient (years)
  • Body temperature : Highest or Lowest recorded body temperature (℃) in ICU D0-D3
  • Heart Rate : Highest recorded heart rate (bpm) in ICU D0-D3

Extracorporeal Organ Support

  • Received pressor/inotrope : IV administration of pressors or inotropes for stabilazation of blood pressure in ICU D0-D3
  • ECMO : Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation

Laboratory Data

  • Bilirubin : Highest recorded value (mg/dL) in ICU D0-D3
  • BUN : Highest recorded value (mg/dL) in ICU D0-D3
  • FiO2 : Highest recorded value (%) in ICU D0-D3
  • pH : Lowest recorded value in ICU D0-D3
  • Platelets : Lowest recorded value (k/uL ✕️ 1000) in ICU D0-D3
  • Hemoglobin : Lowest recorded value (g/dL) in ICU D0-D3
  • Serum Sodium : Lowest recorded value (mmol/L) in ICU D0-D3

AKI Specific Information

  • Admission sCr : Admission creatinine value in ICU D0-D3
  • Hours in ICU : Total hours in ICU D0-D3
  • Maximum KDIGO score : Highest score using the maximum creatinine value in ICU D0-D3
  • Last KDIGO score : Last score using the latest available creatinine in ICU D0-D3
  • Urine Output : Cumulative urine output divided by total number of hours observed in ICU D0-D3

Reference

Prediction of mortality and kidney recovery in critically ill patients with acute kidney injury

Javier A. Neyra, Victor Ortiz-Soriano, Lucas J. Liu, Taylor D. Smith, Xilong Li, Donglu Xie, Beverley Adams-Huet, Orson W. Moe, Robert D. Toto, Jin Chen. "Prediction of mortality and kidney recovery in critically ill patients with acute kidney injury . JOURNAL. DATE. VOLUME."
